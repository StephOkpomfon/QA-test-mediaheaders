Direct Debit Deduction
By signing this Direct Debit Authorization, you have authorized us to arrange funds to be debited from your  credit card or bank account.
Please understand that you will be responsible for any cost, penalty fees, and/or expense incurred if your account does not have sufficient credit balance to cover a direct debit.
Monthly donations enrolled via credit card will be deducted or auto-debited on an ongoing basis per month. Single or one-off donations enrolled via credit card will be deducted on a one-time basis.
Deduction Frequency
The first deduction will be made within 2 to 10 working days upon completion and submission of the donor form.
Subsequently, credit cards will be charged once a month for monthly donors.
Please refer to your credit card statement for confirmation of deductions made.
If you want to give on a recurring basis, please accomplish the form for monthly-gift donations. If you have opted to donate on one-time basis and wish to donate monthly instead, you may contact us at 817-2398 / 4032336 to convert your giving plan.
Confidentiality
We will keep all information in this Direct Debit Authorization confidential. This Program adheres to a strict policy regarding donor privacy. Except for administrative and / or summary reporting purposes, no information in whole or in part, in its paper or electronic format, will be disclosed, used, modified or reproduced to any other person or organization, except in connection with the purposes stated herein.
