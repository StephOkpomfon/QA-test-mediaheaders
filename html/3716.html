UNHCR's voluntary repatriation centers will remain closed on 23 March

Registered Afghan refugees holding Proof of Registration (PoR) or UNHCR-issued refugee cards, who are planning to return to Afghanistan from Pakistan under the UN Refugee Agency’s facilitated voluntary repatriation program are hereby informed, that the UNHCR Voluntary Repatriation Centers (VRCs) in Azakhel in Nowshera and Baleli in Quetta will be closed on account of Pakistan Day on 23 March (Friday) 2018. The centers will reopen on 26 March, Monday.
As per usual practice, Afghan refugees in Pakistan who wish to proceed through the VRCs after the facilitated returns resume are encouraged to contact the following UNHCR helpline numbers, which are operational between 8am to 8pm, before approaching the centres.
The helpline numbers in Pakistan are given below:
